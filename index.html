______The Gunners______

In 1886 this famous club was made up of workers from the Woolwich Arsenal Armament factory,they decided to form a football team.They were called Dial Square it was reference to the sundial on top of the factory enterance.

The appointment of Herbert Chapman 1925 mouled Arsenal into the football club it is today. Chapmans greatest invention was his 3-2-2-3 'WM'formation. It was so successful in shutting out opponents that other English sides adopted to use it. In 1930 was a historic year becuase it was the clubs first major trophy win and also transformed them into the richest and most successful club in the world.
